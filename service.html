Services Offering

Your logistics needs are at the heart of our business, whether you need to move your most complex cargo across continents, or even the smallest of consignments, Sirius Cargo Solutions is in service for you. We are committed to securing our customers' products and their brand intregity by providing continuous training on risk management to our employees to have customers stressless shipping.

01

Air frieght

We deliver on-time shipments, thanks to Sirius Cargo’s extensive global network, service routes and key gateways. All supported by our close carrier relationships across the globe. With our seamless quotation process, and team of industry experts specializing in Air Freight or Air Cargo Services we can offer competitive pricing and space allocation agreements.

  • Export / Import
  • CONSOLIDATED SERVICE DIRECT SHIPMENT
  • OVERNIGHT FREIGHT
  • Reserved Allotment of Space
  • Oversized / DG and HAZ Cargo Handling
02

Ocean freight

We provide dedicated sea freight forwarding services throughout the world and we are here, ready to help you to find the right solution, customized to meet your unique requirements. We cover almost every port in the world, and our sea freight forwarding operates in all major trades.

  • Export / Import
  • LESS THAN CONTAINER LOAD (LCL)
  • Weakly Consolidations
  • FULL CONTAINER LOAD (FCL)
  • Multiple Carrier Service Contract
  • Reefer / Project & Break bulk Cargo
  • CARGO INSURANCE
03

Rail frieght

Sirius Cargo has developed and implemented a roster of specific and sophisticated transport strategies. With growing congestion on the roads and the increasing pressures of climate change, rail is an efficient and environmentally sustainable option for our clients. We integrate rail into our supply chain in a way that makes good economic sense and is most efficient and environmentally minded.

  • Container management
  • Specialized freight
04

Road freight

No problem is too great for Sirius Cargo. We pride ourselves in solving problems by offering customized solutions to each and every customer. With our global network, we are ready to find the best way to move your freight. Our experts are ready to work with you to find excellent options for efficiency and reduced cost.

  • Airport Pickup Less Than a Truckload (LTL)
  • Temperature Controlled Trucks
  • Full Truckload (FTL)
  • CFS Pickup Drayage

Warehousing / Distribution / Trucking

No problem, whatever your warehousing needs, our global network means we have facilities ready to handle your supply chain, whether at origin or destination. By combining shipping with warehousing and distribution and trucking, you will get a benefit from a seamless end-to-end solution. Complexity is reduced, and you gain speed, control and visibility.

  • Security Regulated Warehousing
  • CFS Bounded Warehouse
  • Receiving & Shipping
  • Professional Trucking for Cargo Movement
  • Order Fulfillment
  • Full Range of Scheduled and Time Definitive Deliveries
