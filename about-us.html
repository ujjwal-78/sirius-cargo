Who we are

Sirius Cargo Solutions LLP founded in 2021 by a group of executives that came from leading companies in the international logistics industry. We are the global logistics & freight forwarding services providers, managed by a team with diversified experience and skills in the industry, specializing in innovative shipping management solutions.

We have a network of over 200 agents in aprox. 100 countries across the world, thus providing the maximum coverage and logistics support to our client. We are committed to solve logistics & freight forwarding issues by organizing, leading, controlling and optimizing shipments and information flow which adds a unique value to our clients and provides the ultimate in customer satisfaction.

Why Choose Name Sirius

Sirius is the brightest star in the night sky. Sirius is a binary star and almost twice as bright as Canopus, the next brightest star. Sirius is gradually moving closer to the Solar System, so it is expected to slightly increase in brightness over the next 60,000 years. After that time, its distance will begin to increase, and it will become fainter, but it will continue to be the brightest star in the Earth's night sky for approximately the next 210,000 years.
